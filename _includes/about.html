About Us

We are a local congregation of arabic-speaking believers.

We started meeting in 2005, through the international outreach of First Baptist Church of Lilburn.

We meet together "to equip the saints for the work of ministry, for building up the body of Christ, until we all attain to the unity of the faith and of the knowledge of the Son of God, to mature manhood, to the measure of the stature of the fullness of Christ." (Ephesians 4:12-13)
